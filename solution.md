You will notice some numbers in this solution. They refer to the number of the location where you
have to be in that moment (the numbers are reported in the map). This helps you when you've
been teleported in a random place.

Many events in this adventure are random, making it impossible to elaborate a step-by-step solution.
A good approach is to XSAVE often your position before going places where random things can happen,
e.g. in all the locations where the logmen are. Should things go wrong, you can always XLOAD the
previous position. Only in particular cases you will get stuck in a location because of a random
event: usually, you are relocated in random places, or robbed of some items. You'll easily
recover them on the split-log table (location 42 on the map).

Should you be captured and locked in the logmen's storeroom, which has no exits, you have to
open the trapdoor and go down. You will find yourself in poisoned waters; to increase your chances
and reach the shore, keep heading north. This is valid every time you have to swim, whether you
chose to do it, or you were forced. But remember: you'll NEVER reach the Island swimming!

Keep your strength high with EAT and DRINK commands. You should always have at least 50 strength
points, otherwise some items will fall from your hands. Once on the Island, strength should be more
than 80 (100 if possible), and strength + wisdom should total more than 180, in order to avoid
problems with Omegan and the ghosts in the well of despair. Sometimes, you can TAKE an object not
described in the text, but depicted in the book. Such objects are outlined in yellow in this
walkthrough. You can always choose to see all the hidden items at the beginning of the game.

The verb GIVE is to be conjugated with the given object, omitting the recipient. A second question
will be posed ("to whom?"); you will type in the recipient, using only one word. In this
walkthrough, the GIVE action will be displayed as a single phrase, separated by a comma (e.g., GIVE
APPLE, SNAKE).
